Welcome to RHC Derby

Ramgarhia Hockey Club Derby was formed in 1983. We are based in Sinfin, Derby. The club currently compete in a regional league playing weekly (East Midlands 1). Our club consists of one men’s team and one juniors team. We train on Wednesdays at the City of Derby Academy.

Juniors Wednesday 6:30pm - 7:30pm
Adults Wednesday 6:30pm - 9:00pm
